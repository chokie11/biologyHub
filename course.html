Biology 1: Introduction to Cells

Learn how cells function, grow, reproduce, and generate energy to sustain life.

Course Description

About this course

This course introduces fundamental concepts of cell biology and metabolism. Students explore cell theory, cell structure, cell cycle, and transport mechanisms that maintain homeostasis. The course also covers photosynthesis and cellular respiration, explaining how cells generate energy.

Quarter 1

Cell Biology Lessons

🔬 Cell Theory

History of cell theory and scientists like Hooke and Leeuwenhoek.

🧫 Cell Types

Prokaryotic vs Eukaryotic cells and their structures.

♻️ Cell Cycle

Mitosis, meiosis, and how cells reproduce.

🚚 Cell Transport

Diffusion, osmosis, active and passive transport.

Learning Objectives

What students will learn

  • Explain the modern cell theory
  • Identify cell structures and functions
  • Describe phases of the cell cycle
  • Understand passive and active transport
  • Apply biology concepts to real-life health and environment
